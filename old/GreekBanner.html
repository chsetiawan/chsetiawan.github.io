Programmable LED Banner

I helped build a programmable LED banner with a group of 14 other engineers where we soldered LED strips together to illuminate the Greek letters from behind the fabric.

The LED strips were divided into pieces with section-to-section parallel connections and series connections within the pieces in order to easily debug and replace faulty LED strips.

We attached the ends of the power wires to a central power source, which we connected to an Arduino microcontroller and Bluetooth remote in order to control the LED lights and colors.

The banner has now been used for almost two years during recruitment week and events to showcase past fraternity engineering projects.
