MATLAB Video Processing Player

I worked with two other classmates to create a fully working MATLAB video processing player that can find and play videos from your computer.

We created a working graphical user interface (GUI) and programmed buttons to play, pause, stop, load, rewind, and fast foward the video.

We implemented special features including a real-time scroll bar, a scalable RGB histogram, and FPS/Resolution display.
