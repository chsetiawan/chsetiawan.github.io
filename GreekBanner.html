Multicolor LED Greek Letter Banner

I helped build an LED Greek banner with a group of 14 other engineers where we soldered LED strips together to illuminate the Greek letters from behind the fabric.

The LED strips were divided into sections with section-to-section parallel connections and series connections within the sections in order to easily debug and replace faulty LED strips.

We attached the ends of the power wires to a central power source, which was connected to a Bluetooth remote to control the LED lights and colors.

The banner has now been used for almost two years during recruitment week and events to showcase past fraternity engineering projects.
