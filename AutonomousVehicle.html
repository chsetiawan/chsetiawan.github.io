Autonomous Vehicle Senior Design Project

I'm currently building and designing a self-driving race car with three other engineers for my senior design project.

We used Eagle to design the overall circuit schematic and PCB layout, and our board is currently sent out for fabrication.

We'll be soldering through-hole components, power MOSFETs, low-dropout voltage regulators, a servo motor, a DC motor, and a Bluetooth controller onto our PCB.

We're attaching an OpenMV M7 Camera Microcontroller onto our car and programming it in Python to detect the race track, avoid obstacles, and generate PWM output signals to the servo motor for automated steering.

We've also included options to implement a Tiva C Microcontroller and Linescan Camera in our PCB as well.

We're planning on entering our car into a competition in Spring 2018

This is a two-quarter project spanning from Fall 2017 - Winter 2018, so check back after March 2018 for more updates!
